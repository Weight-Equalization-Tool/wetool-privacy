Privacy Policy for Weight Equalization Tool

Last Updated: October 27, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to the Weight Equalization Tool ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our application.

Information We Do Not Collect

Our application is designed with your privacy in mind. The Weight Equalization Tool:

  • Does not collect any personal information
  • Does not require user registration
  • Does not track user location
  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not store or transmit any data to external servers
  • Does not require internet access

How The App Works

The Weight Equalization Tool:

  1. Processes weight data entirely locally on your device
  2. Performs all calculations on your device
  3. Offers the option to save calculations locally on your device
  4. Functions completely offline without internet connectivity

Data Storage

Local Storage

  • Users can choose to save their weight calculations locally on their device
  • Saved calculations are stored only on the user's device and are not transmitted elsewhere
  • Saved calculations remain on the device until explicitly deleted by the user
  • Users have complete control over their saved data and can delete it at any time
  • We do not have access to any data stored locally on your device

Temporary Data

  • Calculations that are not explicitly saved are held in temporary memory only
  • Unsaved calculations are automatically cleared when the app is closed

Internet Access

  • The app does not require or use internet connectivity
  • No data is transmitted to or from the internet
  • The app functions completely offline

Third-Party Services

  • We do not integrate with any third-party services
  • We do not use any analytics tools
  • We do not incorporate any advertising platforms

Children's Privacy

The Weight Equalization Tool does not collect any personal information from any users, including children under 13 years of age.

User Rights and Control

You have the right to:

  • Choose whether to save calculations locally
  • Access your saved calculations at any time
  • Delete any or all saved calculations from your device
  • Use the app without saving any data

Data Security

  • All saved calculations are stored only on your device
  • We implement industry-standard security practices for local data storage
  • Since data never leaves your device, it cannot be accessed by us or third parties

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date above.

Compliance

This app complies with:

  • Google Play Store Developer Program Policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

Declaration of Local-Only Operation

We explicitly declare that this application:

  • Operates entirely offline
  • Stores data only locally on the user's device when explicitly requested
  • Never transmits any data outside the user's device
  • Gives users complete control over their saved data
  • Cannot and does not access any user data without the user's knowledge

Device Storage Permission

The app requires storage permission only to:

  • Save calculations when requested by the user
  • Access previously saved calculations
  • Delete saved calculations when requested by the user
